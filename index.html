Join Us

Are you a high schooler interested in sailing for La Jolla High School? LJHS Sailing welcomes students of all experience levels to join the team! While we love sailors who already have a bit of sailing experience under their belt, more importantly, the only thing required is an eagerness to learn! More information can be found on this page about team logistics and how to join. We are excited to hopefully have you on our team!
