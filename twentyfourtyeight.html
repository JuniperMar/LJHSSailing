Flying Junior (FJ)

FJs are a double-handed sailboat used in high school and collegiate sailing in the US. Originally, FJs were designed as a training boat for the Flying Dutchman Olympic class. Fjs are 13 ft. 3 in. long and are very maneuverable and fun sailboats.
