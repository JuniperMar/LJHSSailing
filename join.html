Join LJHS Sailing!

Are you a high schooler interested in sailing for La Jolla High School? LJHS Sailing welcomes students of all experience levels to join the team! While we love sailors who already have a bit of sailing experience under their belt, more importantly, the only thing required is an eagerness to learn! Please fill out the Google Form below if you would like to join LJHS Sailing. We are excited to hopefully have you on our team!
