Join LJHS Sailing!

Are you a high schooler interested in sailing for La Jolla High School? LJHS Sailing welcomes students of all experience levels to join the team! While we love sailors who already have a bit of sailing experience under their belt, more importantly, the only thing required is an eagerness to learn! Please fill out the Sign-Up Form below if you would like to join LJHS Sailing. We are excited to hopefully have you on our team!

Practices

Day: Monday and Wednesday

Time: 3/3:30-6/6:30 (depending on the time of year)

Location: Mission Bay Yacht Club

Try before you buy

Send us an email to give us a heads up, then join us for any of our regular Monday/Wednesday practices to ask questions and check the team out before commiting to the entire season!

Non-LJHS Students

The LJHS Sailing Team can accomodate students who do not attend La Jolla High School on a case by case bases. A couple students from Mt. Everest Academy sail for the team as their school does not have a sailing team. Rules regarding students who attend a high school that doesn't have a sailing team or homeschooled students are goverened by PCISA and the decision is theirs. Please contact us so we can help with your specific situation. Learn more about the PCISA rules here.
