About Regattas

There are two primary types of high school sailing regattas that we compete in: SoCals and 10%ers. SoCal Regattas are regional regattas that don't count to our overall rankings. They are great practice with local competition! PCISA 10%ers are larger regattas, each with a specific name. The scores from these regattas count to our team's rankings and will qualify us for the Silver or Gold Pacific Coast Championships. Learn more about following the scores here. With the 2024-25 season completed, the LJHS Sailing Team is ranked 25th out of 69 teams in the PCISA district.
